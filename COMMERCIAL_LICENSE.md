# Commercial Licensing

Mimir is released under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0)**.

The AGPL requires that anyone who distributes or runs a modified version of Mimir as a
network service must publish their modifications under the same license. This is the
right choice for the open source community, but it may not suit every commercial use case.

## When you need a commercial license

A commercial license is available if you:

- Want to integrate Mimir into a proprietary product or service without the AGPL
  obligation to publish your modifications
- Want to redistribute Mimir as part of a closed-source offering
- Need contractual warranties, indemnification, or SLAs beyond what the AGPL provides

## What is always free

- Personal and home use
- Non-profit organizations
- Open source projects (under AGPL terms)
- Internal use where you publish any modifications under AGPL
